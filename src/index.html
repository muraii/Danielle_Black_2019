Hi. I'm Danielle Black.

And I am running for an at-large seat in the Norwood City Council. I want you to know that serving on council is an honor for me. Ever since I was young I’ve wanted to be a voice for people that found themselves silenced. I stood up for the bullied and the meek. As your councilwoman I will make the most sound decisions my ethics and morals allow.

In this tense time for Norwood, there is no place for ego and malice, there is no place for personal interests and secrecy. It’s the light of hope I hear from so many friends and fellow citizens that keeps me pressing on, this hope is what will bring you to the polls, to the meet-and-greets and to choose me to serve on city council. I don’t know everything about local government, but I’m a fast learner and commit to being in the fight to move our city in a positive direction. I commit to giving my time and effort to you, Norwood friends, with hopes of a prosperous and successful future.
